Welcome

This study collects human judgements about reasoning-chain extractions from text. You'll rate the extraction quality and label each conclusion-chain.

What you'll do

  • Generate a unique participant ID.
  • Complete 30 short examples.
  • For each example, choose at least one out of four reasoning classes per conclusion, and rate on a 1–7 Likert scale.

Time: ~15–20 minutes.

Components of the task

Get Started → Generate ID

Data are saved to OSF via DataPipe after each example. Your ID is random and anonymous.
